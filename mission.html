Mission

Mission

We are a non-governmental and non-profit organization with a central mission: to support and promote human development in the world’s poorest countries by promoting the dignity of the human person in all its aspects. We primarily carry out long-term projects with positive developmental impacts and ad hoc emergency interventions as needed. Our development programs fall into various categories and sectors of social life, with full respect for the freedom of thought and religion of others. We call these “chapters”:
International cooperation
- Promotion of education and cultural heritage;
- Implementation of Public health
    (intended as local, one and global health);
- Sustainable use of the environment;
- Fair economic development.
We aim to help communities (especially rural and remote communities) cope with their needs through targeted interventions, providing the necessary means to learn and grow independently. Our values are mutual respect and social justice. Achieving social justice for all is the central point of our global mission for development and human dignity. We operate mainly through International cooperation, cultural bilateral exchanges, E-learning educational support, and scientific methodologies applied to the projects (rigorous evidence-driven evaluation of the projects and their impact, continuous quality implementation of our services).
